# Why Kapwa

Kapwa exists because the Philippines deserves better digital government services.

Too often, public websites feel fragmented, outdated, or hard to use. That creates friction for people who are already trying to complete important tasks: applying for support, accessing records, understanding requirements, or simply finding the right information.

BetterGov is building Kapwa to change that. It is not only a component library. It is a shared design foundation for creating government experiences that are:

- clearer to understand
- more consistent across services
- more accessible to more people
- more worthy of public trust

## Why the name matters

Kapwa is a Filipino value often associated with shared identity, togetherness, and recognizing others as fellow human beings rather than distant users or transactions.

That idea matters in digital public service. Government systems should not feel cold, punishing, or needlessly difficult. They should help people move forward with dignity.

## What Kapwa is trying to do

Kapwa gives teams a common language for:

- visual design
- interface patterns
- token and style architecture
- component usage through Storybook
- documentation of the system's principles and decisions

This site explains the thinking behind Kapwa. Storybook shows the reference implementation. Together, they help move public digital services toward a more humane standard.
