# Overview

Too many online government services still feel harder than they should be. People run into confusing flows, inconsistent interfaces, unclear language, and websites that make important tasks feel more stressful than necessary.

Kapwa exists in response to that reality. These guides explain why the system matters, how teams can adopt it, and how contributors can help build better public digital services in the Philippines.
